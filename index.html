About


Thor Odinson is the Asgardian God of Thunder, superhero, self-proclaimed protector of Earth and the king of Asgard. Thor made a name for himself as the mightiest warrior on his homeworld and subsequently became well known for his actions on Earth. He's a BEAST.

Abilities

➤ Stormbreaker

Wields the enchanted Uru-forged axe Stormbreaker, which grants mastery over the elements of thunder & lightning, as well as the ability to fly & open interdimensional gateways.

➤ Strength

Superhuman strength, speed, endurance & resistance to injury

➤ Asgardian

Member of the otherworldly & virtually immortal Asgardian race
